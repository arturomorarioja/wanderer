About Us

Our History

The present collection emerged as an added value service within Wanderer Publishing. After a 2012 survey where our readers showed a great interest in foreign countries, we decided to create a top quality travel guide collection that met the high standards the company is accustomed to.

Our intention is to cover all the major touristic destinations in the world. Thanks to the extraordinary work of our editorial department we hope to do so by the end of 2019.

Editorial Team

Niels I. Holm Editor-in-chief

Niels has been in the publishing business for over thirty years. His wide experience covers diverse local, national, and international media like The Aarhus Standard, Katter og Hunder, Nordic Birdspotting, or Brussels Digest. His in-depth knowledge of the business and attention to detail allow him to steer the publishing ship seamlessly and efficiently.

Michelle J. Toft Photography

Her care of colours and framing has made of Michelle one of the most valued photographers of our time. Along the last decade she has coined a unique style based on original lens combinations, an agressive treatment of saturation, and the ability to deliver pictures that represent life in an almost expressionist way.

Selma O. Thomsen Graphic Designer

Out travel guides would not be half as easily recognizable as they are were it not for Selma's craftswomanship. Her one of a kind layout combining innovation and ease of access is the spark that lights the content of the collection.

Art Berry Author

The renowned author of countless travel journals and books, Art's deep knowledge of international cultures and mastery of the wrriten word lies at the heart of his work's top quality.
